Executive Summary

The US boutique fashion market is projected to grow at an impressive 12.8% year-over-year, presenting a wealth of opportunities for boutique owners who are ready to harness the power of curated brands. These brands have consistently outperformed national chains, particularly in the areas of customer loyalty and Average Order Value (AOV).

Market Deep Dive

The boutique market is forecasted to hit $65 billion in 2024, driven by a growth rate of 12.8% annually. Mobile-first shopping has taken the lead, constituting 68% of transactions in this sector. Furthermore, three major consumer trends are reshaping the landscape:

  • 78% of boutique shoppers now find new brands on Instagram.
  • 61% show a preference for limited-edition drops over evergreen collections.
  • 53% consider sustainability a significant factor influencing their purchase decisions.

Consumer Persona Analysis
